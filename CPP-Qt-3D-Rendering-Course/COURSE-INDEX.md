**Total Lessons**: 180 lessons
**Focus Areas**: C++ Programming, Qt Framework, 3D Graphics, Performance Optimization

## Learning Path Recommendations

### For Complete Beginners
Start with Part 1 (Lessons 1-20), then Part 2 (21-40), then choose your specialization.

### For Experienced C++ Developers
Skip to Part 4 (Qt) or Part 6 (3D Rendering) based on your interests.

### For Game Developers
Focus on Parts 1-3, 6-8, and 9 for complete graphics programming knowledge.

### For UI Developers
Focus on Parts 1-2, 4-5 for Qt expertise.
